GIVE TO LIVE CELL (G2LC)
INFORMATION

We feel glad to present before you our own social cell g2lc. It was formed in 2011 with a mission to evolve as the center of philanthropic activities, providing basic amenities and education to all the needy children thus creating sustainable manpower for the all round development of our nation. We wish to work collectively for social welfare of the nation and give our best management and technical efforts in eradicating illiteracy from the society and preserving our ecological beauty, thus making the country developed in real sense. g2lc formed its three sub cells

1) Try Eco
2) Each One-Teach One
3) Contribute

Remember "A candle loses nothing if it is used to light the other one"
