CADSTRIFE
INTRODUCTION

The fifth golden edition of CAD competition has finally begun in Prajwalan ’14 in the foremost war CADSTRIFE. The most eminent position in design world can be achieved through the integration of various CAD softwares. So the challenge is here and you all engineers are most welcome to sustain in design era of THE CAD COMBAT…CADSTRIFE and prove your talent.
RULES

    AUTOCAD – CIVIL ENGINEERING 2D
  • Round 1:-Develop 2D plan in 15 minutes.
  • Round 2:-Draw a sectional view in 15/20 minutes.
  • Round 3:-Draw plan, elevation and section in 1 hour.
    AUTOCAD – CIVIL ENGINEERING 3D
  • Round 1:- 25 MCQs based on building bye-laws.
  • Round 2:- To develop a 3D structure.
    AUTOCAD – MECHANICAL ENGINEERING
  • Round 1:- To draw basic shapes in 10 minutes.
  • Round 2:- To draw front view and side view.
  • Round 3:- To develop all views.
    PRO-E (Creo also) / CATIA
  • Round 1:- To draw basic shapes in 10 minutes.
  • Round 2:- To develop complex shapes in 20 minutes.
  • Round 3:- To develop assembly design in 1 hour.
INSTRUCTION

  • The registration fee will be Rs. 60/- per member.
  • All participants will be awarded with the certificates.
  • The group consists of students of either two different or same institutes.
  • The rules may be subjected to change according to circumstances and decisions.
  • All team members must come with college ID cards.
