CIRCUIT DESIGNING
INTRODUCTION
    Beyond the boundaries of books, beyond boundaries theories here comes practical opportunity To show case your talent in TRONIKA (Circuit Designing Competition)
  • There will be three groups in this event Group-A consist of 1styr only Group-B consist of polytechnic, & 2ndyr participants and Group-C consist of 3rd& 4th yr participants.
  • The competition will be held in Three round
  • First round is Qualification round in which we will provide you MCQ’s there is time limit to solve it. On the basis of score participants will short listed for Second round
  • Second round we will provide you MCQ’s there is time limit to solve it. On the basis of score participants will short listed for Third round
  • Third round is circuit mounting in which we will give you logical questions according to that you have to mount circuit in given time limit (components will be provided by us)
  • Entry fees:- 100 rupees per team or 50 rupees per candidate or maximum two members in a team. Limited entries are here.
  • There is separate prizes for Each groups that is One Winner and One Runner (for Group-A there is only Winner prize)
RULES

  • In 1st& 2ndround we will note that in what time they solve paper.
  • We will short the participants in 1st& 2ndround according to their marks also.
  • In 3rdround we will also note that in what time short list participants solve the circuit.
  • Participants must show circuit the output of the circuit.
  • Also they must explain circuit and according to we will ask few questions.
  • We will also note that in what time participants analyses their circuit.
