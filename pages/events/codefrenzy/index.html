CODE MANIA
INTRODUCTION

  • Code Mania is C & C++ language based event
  • Total 2 rounds will be conducted.
  • Round- 1 (Elimination round):
    • Participants will be provided with a pdf of 10 questions which will be downloadable at www.prajwalan.org in Code Mania Uploads section.
    • Participants have to code and mail the programs in a span of 18 hours at address codefrenzy@prajwalan.org
    • Time of coding will start from 4pm on 22ND February to 10am on 23rd February.
    • This round is OPEN-BOOK round & from this round top 20 teams will be selected for Round-2.
  • Round-2 :
    • Round-2 will be on 25th February & will involve on-spot coding which will be conducted in our college computer lab.
    • Participants will have to code 5 programs within 3 hours.
    • Team consists of maximum 3 team members & minimum 2 team members.
REGISTRATION

  • The registration fees will be as follows
  • Team of 2: Rs. 140/-
  • Team of3: Rs. 210/-
  • Registration will be done on or before 21st February before 12:00 pm
RULES

  • No participants will be registered after 12pm on 21st of February.
  • The participants are expected to submit their answers for online test before 10am on 23rd February. Replies after 10 am will not be considered.
  • The participants have to report for Round- 2 at 10 am on 25th February sharp.
  • The candidates reporting for Round -2 must have their identity cards and registration receipt.
  • In case of tie, program having less execution time will be preferred.
  • The participant will be judge on the following criteria:
    • Maximum accuracy
    • Efficiency in coding
    • Minimum execution time
