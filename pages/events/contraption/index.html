TASK
MAIN TASKS(Any One):
    1. Prepare a Hot Coffee or
    2. Cut the thread into 4 equal parts
Intermediate Task: (atleast 2 task compulsory)
    1) Paint /trace 50 on a A4 size paper
    2) Fold paper in 2 equal half
    3) Split water into 3 parts (in different bottles)
    4) Generation of electricity
    5) Use the non-conventional energy.
Bonus Tasks: (atleast 1task compulsory)
    1)Use of Magnetic Energy
    2)Ignite a candle and Extinguish it.
RULES

  • Participants have to do any one of the two mentioned end task.
  • Space of 5ftx5ft will be provided to each team.
  • Maximum 6 members are allowed per team.
  • Maximum 5 hand touches are allowed
  • Use of highly explosive material is not allowed.
  • Participants should bring their own extension box.
  • Points of innovation will be given by the judges.
  • Extra points will be awarded to participants using “Go Green” theme
  • Weightage of intermediate task is different and defined in scoring criteria.
  • Decision of judges will be final.
