GOOGLER
INTRODUCTION

      "An army of soldiers without a war engineer is a piece of flesh which can easily bleed.Lot of conspiracies go around at the time of wars which need strategic planning to outmaneuver the opponent. Hoping to be victorious at Google fields without proper planning won’t mitigate opponent’s chances of victory.To defeat the other kingdoms, solve the puzzle with the most efficient algorithm with your friend google in your hand."

Googler is an Event which consist of various activities need to be executed with the use of any application related to google. This event is distributed in three rounds. Round 1 will consist of 10 questions which are to be search on google search engine within given time. Top 20 teams are selected for next round. Round 2 and round 3 will be surprised for eligible participant. Rules of Round 2 and round 3 will be informed to the selected teams after results of 1st round.

REGISTRATION FEES:Rs 60/- per Team

(2 Members per Team)

RULES

Round 1 will consist of 10 questions which are to be search on google search engine within given time. Top 20 teams are selected for next round.
There will be total 3 rounds in which top 20 teams are to be selected for 2nd round. These 20 teams will be given some tasks/games based on google search engine from which next 5 teams will be selected for last 3rd round. Round 3rd will be declared to only those 5 teams which contain some more tricky tasks based on google. So you just to use your surfing skill and make your way to win.
