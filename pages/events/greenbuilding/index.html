GREEN STRUCTURES (GREEN BUILDING)
INFORMATION


As India’s population moves increasingly to being urban population, great challenges of sustainable building and development face the human race. Buildings can no longer afford to be wasteful. The only way left for cities to grow is to do so in a sustainable manner. Are you ready to face this challenge?
All you have to do is gather some colleagues and find out all the ways you can make a building sustainable. Put them all in your model and be the Champions of ‘Green Building’…!!!
So wake up and design a building of human dreams!!!
RULES
  • The event will be conducted in one single round.
  • Participants must build a building based on the given criteria.
  • Plan of building prepared in Auto-CAD or other relevant software is mandatory.
  • Buildings must include as many of facilities as possible.
  • Participants should build a model of their building and bring it on the event day.
  • Each team consists of maximum five members.
  • The buildings will be judged on the basis of the criteria mentioned in the problem statement.
  • ENTRY FEES: - Rs. 300/- TEAM. (Max 5 members / team).
  • LAST DATE: - for abstract is 20th February 2014.
