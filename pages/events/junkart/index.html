JUNK ART
INTRODUCTION

It’s great opportunity for all creative minds to spark out their artistic ideas and implement them. In this electronic world large amount of e-waste is thrown off which cannot be recycled, causing pollution. Our aim is to create well from waste. Here we will give you a chance to think out of the box and create something from the junk provided in the creative and innovative way.
So, you’ll be left to some junk, scraps and yourself to test your creative and innovative skills to the furthest extreme. Finally prove that…………One Person’s Junk is Another’s Junk Art!!!!!!!
RULES

    We will be conducting three respective rounds:
  • Round 1: Participants will be provided with certain e-waste and they have to create number of objects in a specified time. The model will be shown prior to the event.
  • Round 2: Participants qualifying the first round will be shown the model of an object larger in size as compared to first round and has to make it as soon as possible (perfectly).
  • Round 3 Now the participants qualifying round 2 will be given a theme and they have to implement their own ideas and make an art out of it.
NOTE

  • Each team consists of two members.
  • Each team will be provided with equal amount of e-waste (junk).
  • Eliminations will be there after each round.
