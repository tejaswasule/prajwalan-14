JUNKYARD WARZ
INFORMATION

What would happen if you crossed How Stuff Works with a game show? Quite likely, you would get something along the lines of ”Junkyard Wars". Racing against the clock, many teams scrap together working machines to carry out a particular mission. At the end of each show, the different teams pit their creations against each other to find out who has put together the best solution to the producers' challenge.
To fans of all ages, the formula is an ideal mesh of education and entertainment. The show has the same "real people" appeal of hit reality shows like "Survivor," but contestants compete with pure mental prowess and ingenuity, rather than alliances and plots.
RULES

1. A valid id card of your college is a must.
2. The teams can have members of different colleges.
3. For eliminations teams have to appear for different round of task as single entity.
4. Each team can consist of 3 members.
5. The organizers reserve all rights to disqualify any team for usage of unfair means.
6. In case of any discrepancies, decision of the organizers shall be considered final and binding for all.
7. The participants must adhere to rules and regulations of Govt. College of Engineering Amravati, Maharashtra.
8. The teams have to complete the prescribed task in given time and using only the materials provided.
9. The teams of three member must have receipt before taking part in competition. Kindly, bring your receipt during event which is mandatory for each team.
10. No outside junk is allowed to use if anyone found with outside junk may lead to disqualification at that instant.
11. Each team should concern about their task. Do not disturb and interfere other team.
12. No extra time will be given to any team .Complete your task within stipulated time.
13. Anyone found with violation of rule may directly lead to disqualification.
INSTRUCTION

Techfest ‘Prajwalan XIV’ Unleash your wild creativity at Techfest ‘Prajwalan’ 2014 and join the fun in creating amazing stuff out of scrap for one and all, the activity is an ideal mesh of education and entertainment. Techfest ‘Prajwalan ’brings to you the 'Junkyard Wars'. Come to showcase your talent and creativity and mould the backyard junk into something enviable

The event consists of three rounds:

Eliminations - All the teams have to first pass the hurdle of eliminations. The teams will perform first round of task within stipulated time. It will check the basic concepts of engineering standard vital for other rounds.

Second stage eliminations – Top 50 teams after eliminations will move to round two. Here they have to complete a given task. It will check their ability for final round. The problem statement will be provided at the time of event.

Finals - A specific number of teams selected after second stage will wage the "Junkyard Wars". For this round, teams will be required to make a machine, out of the provided junk, which satisfies the given problem statement, which will be announced at the time of the event.
