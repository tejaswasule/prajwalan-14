COUNTER STRIKE

RULES:
  • Maximum number of participants per team is 4.
  • Matches will be 4 players (a team) vs. 4 players (opponent team).
  • Upon discovery of any player committing any violations regarded as unfair play, that player and his team will be disqualified from the tournament.
  • Use of cheats are strictly prohibited.
  • Other rules will be notified ON SPOT.
  • Default controls will be used.
  • Use of your own gears, gaming pads and sound devices is acceptable only if allowed by the LAN-gaming committee.
  • All decisions of LAN gaming convener will be acceptable to all players.
  • Gaming bugs and errors are not responsibility of LAN gaming committee, in such situation decisions of convener will be acceptable to all teams.
  • Participant who will not follow above rules will be disqualified with his team.
  • On spot registration is open for all games including CS 1.6.
