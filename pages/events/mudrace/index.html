GRID SOLVER
INTRODUCTION

  • Team must build an autonomous robot which will reach the END point from the START point solving the GRID by the principle of line follower.
RULES

  • Each team may consist of maximum 4 members with entry fees of Rs 400/-
  • A team may consist of students from different colleges and each member should bring their college ID proof.
  • Maximum 3 trials will be given to each team and each trial will be given maximum 3 minutes.
  • In case if no team reaches the END, the winner will be decided according to the performance of robot.
  • The size of robot should be less than 30*30*30 cm and weight should be less than 3 kg.
  • Organizers may change the rule as per situation
  • Organizers decision will be the final decision.
TASK

  • The robot will start from the START point and have to reach the END point.
  • The team reaching the END in minimum time including the BONUS points and PENALTY points will be the winner.
  • The extra points will be given if robot passes through the BONUS zone (B.Z) and points will be deducted if robot passes through the DANGER zone(D.Z).
  • BONUS SCHEME: 5, 15 or 25 points for one, two or three BONUS ZONE(B.Z) passed respectively.
  • PENALTY SCHEME: 10 points per DANGER ZONE (D.Z) will be deducted.
  • No extra points will be rewarded if robot passes through same BONUS ZONE (B.Z) more than once.
