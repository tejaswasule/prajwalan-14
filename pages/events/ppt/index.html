PAPER PRESENTATION
INTRODUCTION

    Pen down your research work and present it effectively. Cover all the engineering aspects of your topic and you can be one of the short-listed finalists based on your abstract. The paper will be judged on the content, its originality, flow of the presentation, communication skills and co-ordination among team members.
  • Be Technical, present urself,
  • Be a Geek, win Prizes !!!
    • RULES

    • Each team can have a maximum of 2 members.
    • The theme of the paper must be based on one of the mentioned topics.
    • Paper should not exceed 12 pages.
    • Paper must be in IEEE format. (Paper should be essentially in a two column format; Font Style: Times New Roman; Font Size: 12; Headings: Bold and font size 14)
    • Last date for the submission of paper is 22nd February 2014.
    • The teams will get 8+2 minutes for presentation followed by question and answer session.
    • Bring your college I-Card & receipt on event day.
    • Soft-copies of the submitted paper and PowerPoint presentations must be in the 97-2003 format.
    • Only pure technical papers with valid reference would be considered for short listing.
    • Any violation of the rules may result in rejection of the paper.
    • For any unusual circumstance, final decision of the organizers and judges will be binding to all.
    • Final round rules to be sent as a circular to the selected candidates.
    • Registration fees 300/- per team.
