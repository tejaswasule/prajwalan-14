PRAJWALAN CUP
INTRODUCTION

    "You will never win if you never begin" - Helen Rowland

    Inviting the colleges to compete and defeat the opponents to win the coveted Prajwalan cup!
    Prajwalan cup is a section that pits your physical, technical, creative and artistic skills all together for the array of competitions . With no pre registrations and pre-requisites, enjoy the festive excitement and adrenaline gushing events.
    So join the rail and witness the vigorous clash of the deserving colleges for the most desired title of Prajwalan’14!!
STRUCTURE

    You can earn points in two ways:
    1)Participation points:
    Show up your technical expertise by participating in the various mind boggling events and in a way contributing for your college as well!
    2)Team Events:
    Build up your college team and enter into the final series of competitions for the ultimate hunt of the cup!!
RULES

  • Only one team per college will be allowed.
  • Team should be of maximum 20 members.
  • Members will be decided on the criteria of first come first service.
CHAMPIONS

  • 2012 – Prof. Ram Meghe institute of technology, Amravati.

  • 2013 – Sipna college of engineering and technology, Amravati.
