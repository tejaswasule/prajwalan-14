INTRODUCTION

Prajwalan Times is great platform to bring out the journalist in you. Event is providing you the opportunities to showcase your journalism skills. Prajwalan Times is open to all engineering branches and other students as well. Event will consist of three rounds as explained below:
  • Round 1 : In the first round, participants will have to present a typical news report and submit news article on the first day of event for one of the topic which will be displayed on Prajwalan website. Each participant will be provided with 6 minute for presentation.
  • Round 2 : In the second round called “Breaking News”, short-listed candidates will have to cover the Live Event. Each team will be sent to one of the event going on in the campus and they will have to perform live reporting for that event. Team have to submit report clipping recorded with help of mobile, camcorder etc., at the end of round. Each team will have 15-20 min to perform this task.
  • Round 3 : In the final round, short-listed candidates will be hosting a “Talk Show”. One of the member from each team will have to take the interview. Each participant will get time of 8 minutes for the interview.
RULES

  • Each team can have maximum 2 members.
  • No language barrier. One can use Hindi, English or Marathi for various rounds.
  • Marks will be deducted for not following rules and crossing time limits.
  • Certificates will be presented to only those participant who will be attending the event.
  • Any further changes, if made will be updated from time to time.
  • ENTRY FEES: Rs 150 per Team (Max 2 Members)
INSTRUCTION

  • Event will be of two days.
  • Participants need to carry video recording device e.g. Cam,mobile
