DEATH RACE
INTRODUCTION

The simple manual robot has to complete the race in minimum time overcoming the obstacles.
RULES

  • Each team may consist of maximum 4 members with entry fees of Rs 400/- .
  • A team may consist of students from different colleges and each member should bring their college ID proof.
  • 32V variable power supply will be provided by organizers.
  • Robot should have minimum 20 ft. wire for smooth working.
  • The size of robot should be less than 30*30*30 cm and weight should be less than 5 kg.
  • Organizers may change the rule as per situation
  • Organizers decision will be the final decision.
