VIRTUAL-PC
INFORMATION

“VIRTUAL-PC” is an online computer based event. This event provides an opportunity to show your computer knowledge as well as business knowledge. The student related to any field can be participating in this event.
The Event structure will be as follows:

Round I
In this round task will be given to the participants to assemble a computer in the given budget. The task will be uploaded on the 20th Feb on the subdomain www.vpc.prajwalan.org

Round II
In the second round the task will be given on spot to the 10% selected participants from round I on 25th – 26th Feb.
RULES

  • The team will be minimum of one member and maximum of two members.
  • The participants username will be his/her surname.firstname (e.g-wasule.tejas).
  • The participants password will be his/her receipt number.
  • If the team will be of two members the username will be the name of one of the participant.
INSTRUCTION

  • The Entry fees for the Event is Rs. 80(for single member) and Rs. 150 (For two members).
  • The Round I will be from 20th Feb up to 23th Feb (12 a.m).
  • The selected participants from Round I will be convey by message on 24th Feb .
  • The Round II will be held on 25th – 26th Feb.
  • The time and venue will be inform to the selected participants via message.
