VIRTUAL STOCK
INTRODUCTION

  • The Virtual Stock is an event cum intermediate stage of Business Plan Competition.
  • The Plans selected for the presentation round will have to market their product or service as it is done in Share Market.
  • The Contestant participating in Virtual Stock will be given virtual money to invest in the shares.
  • The contestant having maximum profit at the end of the event will be declared as winner of the Virtual Stock event.
  • Any student can participate in the Virtual Stock whether he/she is member of B-plan team or not by paying the entry fees and can earn maximum profit and may become winner
RULES

  • Each Team/Plan will have its pre-defined share value.
  • Invest RS 10000 virtual money, you have been provided to buy/sell shares of various companies.
  • Buying/selling is considered as one transaction.
  • You can buy/sell only 30 shares/per company in one transaction.
  • You can buy/sell only 100 shares of a given company.
  • Share Rates would vary every half hour.
  • For winning, a minimum of 5 transactions are required.
  • (For B-Plan contestant)The team/plan having maximum share value and profit at end of the event will be getting maximum no. of marks of the 20% of total presentation marks.
  • The rise or fall of the share value would be subject to the decision of the jury and judges only.
INSTRUCTION

  • The entry fees for Virtual Stock is Rs.40.
  • The Event will be on 25th feb form 10 am onwards.
