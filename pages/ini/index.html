"We generate wealth for the people. What comes from the people must,
to the extent possible, therefore get back to the people."

Social initiative is basically a concept whereby institution decides voluntarily to contribute to a better society and a cleaner environment. Social initiative is represented by the contributions undertaken by students to society through various activities. Although the prime goal of institution is to impart technical education, institution can at the same time contribute to social and environmental objectives by integrating social responsibility and technology.
